A flagship 6-week live consulting engagement by the Social & Business Club (SocBiz), IIT Roorkee, for one of India's largest mall developers. Tasked with reversing stubbornly low food-court conversion rates in Tier-2 cities despite near-full footfall recovery, the team designed and piloted high-ROI interventions that lifted weekend conversion by 16–22%, average order value by 18%, and sales per sq ft by 24% within 60 days.
